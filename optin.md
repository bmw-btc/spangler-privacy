## How to Subscribe

Text **START** to **+1 (888) 773-1332** to subscribe to Spangler Messaging Service notifications from Brock Capital LLC.

You will receive a confirmation message:

> "You're subscribed to Spangler Messaging Service by Brock Capital LLC. Expect 5-100 msgs/day. Reply STOP to cancel, HELP for support. Msg & data rates may apply."

- Daily productivity briefings (morning and evening)
- System status alerts and health monitoring notifications

## Opt-Out
Reply **STOP** to +1 (888) 773-1332 at any time to stop all messages.
